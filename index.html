Our Story

From a backroom hustle to a trusted local hub.

2014

Started From the Backrooms

Internet Smart Hub began small — fixing computers and phones out of the backrooms, built on word of mouth and getting the job done right.

2019

Went Mobile

We took the workshop on the road, bringing repairs and support directly to customers wherever they needed us.

2025

Opened Our Alexandra Shop

In July 2025, we planted our flag with a proper base of operation in Alexandra — a home for everything we do.

Today

Steady Growth, Strong Delivery

We keep growing, one satisfied customer at a time — the same honest, reliable service that got us here.
