Privacy Policy

What we collect through this site, why, and how to reach us about it.

What we collect

When you use a quote, order or inquiry form on this site, we collect what you type in: your name, contact number, and email address where asked, plus the details of what you're requesting (e.g. device brand/model, items selected, message). We don't collect anything else automatically — no tracking cookies, no ad networks.

Why we collect it

Only to respond to your quote, order or inquiry — to work out pricing, contact you back, and keep a record of what was agreed if you come into the shop.

Where it goes

Submissions are stored in a private database/spreadsheet accessible only to the shop owner, and may trigger an email notification to the shop. Nothing is sold, shared, or used for marketing to third parties.

Your ticket & QR code

Each ticket is also saved to your own device's local browser storage so you can view it again on the same device, and includes a QR code you can show in-store — this QR code just encodes your own ticket details, nothing more.

Getting your information removed

Contact us any time at internetsmarthub@gmail.com or 069 730 4534 and we'll delete your submitted details on request.
